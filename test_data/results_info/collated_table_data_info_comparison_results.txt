Data Presentation Note:

  - Time (T(s)) is averaged over ALL trials (reflecting timeouts).
  - Makespan (MS) and Collisions (CO) are averaged *only* over successful trials.
  - A '-' for MS/CO indicates no successful trials in group with valid data for that metric.
  - A '-' for T(s) indicates no trials in group provided valid time data.
